CNG Kit
  • Always choose an RTO-approved CNG kit and retrofitter.
  • Make sure the CNG kit is compatible with your vehicle.
  • Do check the quality of the accessories used.
  • Make sure your CNG kit is fitted correctly.
Cylinder
  • Ensure the cylinder is approved by PESO.
  • Check the hydro-testing validity.
  • It should be securely mounted with proper clamps.
RC and Insurance
  • Update your RC with CNG endorsement.
  • Inform your insurer about the CNG installation.
  • Ensure policy covers CNG-related claims.
